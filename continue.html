What we will do

After completion of the beginner questions, we will build on the knowledge you obtained from practice. We will continue with basic questions after testing your grammar skills in the audio section. In the audio section, complete sentences with appropiate punctuation and grammar are expected. Incorrect grammar or missing punctuation will cause the entire answer to be marked incorrect. For the following section single word answers are expected. Capitalization does not matter, but appropiate spelling is expected.
