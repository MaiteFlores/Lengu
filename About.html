Our Mission

Whether you have little to no Spanish skills or have grown up speaking the language, this website will help give insight into what is your current comprehensive level. With simple activities which will test your listening comprehension skills, basic translation skills, and simple communication skills, we will evaluate how well you understand the concepts necessary for basic communication is a Spanish environment.

For these activities you will need access to headphones and a Spanish keyboard. Some lessons will require careful listening and writing. We will be testing you on grammar, spelling, and listening comprehension on various exercises. Although it may sound intimidating, our goal is for the exercises to be practice, rather than lessons or actual tests. There are absolutely no limit to retries of the exercises and it is strongly encouraged to retake exercises which posed difficulty to the viewer. Above all else we want the viewer to have as much fun as we did creating this website!
